Everything you want to know about Makeki Bakery.

Ordering and Delivery

Custom cake orders must be placed at least 48 hours in advance. Fill in our contact form, select Custom Cake Enquiry, and describe your design, size, and dietary needs. We call back within 2 hours and collect a 50% deposit.

Yes. Flat fee of Ksh 300 for orders above Ksh 2,000. Delivery zones: Westlands, Karen, Kilimani, Lavington, CBD. Order by 5 pm for next-day 7-10 am delivery.

We accept M-Pesa (Till No. 123456), cash, and bank transfer for large orders. No card payments in-store at this time.

Products and Ingredients

We bake a dedicated gluten-free range every Tuesday and Friday using certified gluten-free flour. Our kitchen does handle wheat flour so we cannot guarantee zero cross-contamination for people with coeliac disease.

Wheat from Eldoret Grain Millers. Butter and dairy from Kiambu County cooperatives. Sri Lankan cinnamon, Madagascan vanilla, and fresh fruit from Wakulima Market in Nairobi.

3-4 days at room temperature in a cloth bag. Do not refrigerate — cold air dries the crumb. Frozen and sliced it keeps for up to 3 months.
